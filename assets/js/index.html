Ethereum Black Speed

Get faster download speeds and ETHB token rewards.

Ethereum Black Speed is available as a feature of Ethereum Black and µTorrent Web, as well as µTorrent Classic.

With Ethereum Black Speed enabled, downloaders will have the option of providing tokens to uploaders in exchange for faster download speed. By introducing an incentive of earning ETT tokens, uploaders have a reason to seed files longer, and to dedicate more of their bandwidth and storage to other torrent clients in the network. ETHB, which serves as a crypto torrent token in EtherTorrent Speed, is responsible for powering a healthier Ethereum Black protocol.

How it Works

Enable ETHB and enjoy faster download speeds.

Step 1 - Enable your wallet

When downloading or upgrading to a new version of EtherTorrent or µTorrent Web, Daitorrent, Rippletorrent or µTorrent Classic, the torrent software will be enabled with a wallet and ETT token balance.

Step 2 - Wallet Password

Create a strong password and save it in a safe place. We do not have access to your password and will not be able to restore it. Losing your password may result in losing access to your funds.

Step 3 - Upload and Earn ETT

When a torrent file finishes downloading, you can automatically earn EtherTorrent (ETT) by seeding. The integrated wallet allows for secure storage of your ETT, which you can send to other wallets.
